Background

SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

The Dragon spacecraft is capable of carrying up to 7 passangers to and from Earth Orbit, and beyond. It is the only spacecraft currently flying that is capabale of returning significant amounts of cargo to Earth, and is the first private spacecraft to take humans to the Space Station.
